## Meta Writing Rules

- Keep `title` short enough to fit on result cards and gallery detail.
- Keep `subtitle` under one line on mobile.
- Keep `caption` to one sentence.
- Put creator/tool credits in `credit`, not in `caption`.
- Do not change `chapter_id` or `ending_id` to force a wrong file into another slot.
