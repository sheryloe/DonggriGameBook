## QA Rules

- Chapter briefing art, map art, and result art for the same chapter must feel like the same place and weather window.
- An ending thumbnail must read as the same ending as its full art at a glance.
- An ending video poster must match the silhouette and lighting direction of the ending full art.
- If a dropped image looks like another chapter or another ending, treat it as a wrong asset and replace it.
