# Design QA Checklist

- Chapter briefing image, map hero, and result card must read as the same chapter.
- Ending thumb, ending full art, and ending video poster must share the same silhouette and mood.
- Do not mix warm hopeful lighting into `Ashen Escape`.
- Do not make `Mirror Witness` look cleaner or safer than `Signal Keepers`.
- If an image is missing, confirm the `X placeholder` is visible and layout still holds.
- If only a video exists, confirm the poster card still renders correctly before playback.
- If only a thumb exists for an ending, keep the card readable but treat the detail view as missing.
